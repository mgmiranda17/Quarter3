HOW TO PREVENT FIRES

Fire can happen anytime and anywhere without the proper care of materials at home, not thinking carefully on what you are using or rather not being aware of your surroundings. Here we wil be telling you the causes of fire and how to prevent it.

# Example Cause Prevention
1 Fire is mostly cause by not unplugging electronics inside the house that are not in use. If plugged too often it might heat up and start a fire or rather for the electronic to explode. Always unplug things when they are not in use to avoid house fires.
2 Smoking is dangerous to your health as well as your surroundings. Cigarettes that are lit if dropped can cause a fire to spread around the house The only way to prevent this is to not smoke in the building or house you are in. Quitting smoking is also a much better option.
3 Lighted up candles are dangerous anywhere cause of the risk of it falling and spreading around. Keep candles away from anything flammable or rather use a battery operated light source such as flashlights.
4 Faulty wiring is one of the causes of fire as well and it is because households have ignored the wiring inside which they dont know is actually prone to fire. Once you see wiring that is unusual or not fixed, Call for an electrician immediately instead of waiting for it to cause a house fire.
