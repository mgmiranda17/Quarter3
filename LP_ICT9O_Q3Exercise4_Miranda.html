Okada Manila

The Okada Manila is one of the most luxurious hotels I have stayed in. It is the largest integrated resort in Manila with a Beach club and a VIP area. They offer shuttle services to tour you around the hotel and bring you anywhere within the mall. Different Variety of Foods to choose from such as Japanese Food, Indian Food, Chinese Food and Traditional Filipino Food. I really suggest going here if you want to have time away from reality because this place is truly magical

Amenities and Facilities in Okada Manila: Cove Manila, Swimming Pool, 5 star dining restaurants, Fountain Show, Casino, Kids Club, Spa and Wellness Center, Garden View, In Mall Shops.
